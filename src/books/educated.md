# Educated

by **Tara Westover**

A powerful memoir about growing up in a survivalist family in rural Idaho and eventually pursuing an education. The book explores themes of family loyalty, education, and the transformative power of knowledge.

Westover's writing is gripping and her story is both heart-wrenching and inspiring. She writes with such honesty about her complicated relationship with her family and the pain of choosing education over her family's way of life.
