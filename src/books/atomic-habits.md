# Atomic Habits

by **James Clear**

An incredibly insightful book about building good habits and breaking bad ones. The author provides practical frameworks for habit formation, emphasizing the importance of small, incremental changes.

## Key Takeaways

- Small changes can make a big difference
- Focus on systems, not goals
- Your habits shape your identity
- Make good habits obvious, attractive, easy, and satisfying
