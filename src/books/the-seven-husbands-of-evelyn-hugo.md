# The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo

by **Taylor Jenkins Reid**

A mesmerizing novel about a reclusive Hollywood icon who finally decides to tell her life story to an unknown journalist. The narrative masterfully weaves through decades of glamour, secrets, and complicated relationships.

A compelling story of ambition, love, and the price of fame. The characters are beautifully developed and the plot twists keep you engaged until the very end.
